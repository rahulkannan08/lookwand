## Overall Messaging Strategy

### Core Principles:
1. **Human-First Language** - Emphasize that people (models, stylists, photographers) are essential, not optional
2. **Efficiency Without Sacrifice** - AI accelerates workflow without compromising authenticity
3. **Risk Reduction** - Help brands make smarter decisions with data, not gut feelings
4. **Sustainability Angle** - Reduce overproduction and waste in fashion industry
5. **Empowerment Over Replacement** - Technology amplifies human creativity

### Tone:
- Confident but not arrogant
- Technical but accessible
- Progressive but grounded in reality
- Aspirational but practical

### Key Differentiators to Emphasize:
- ✅ Built on real model photoshoots (not synthetic avatars)
- ✅ Test-before-produce workflow (reduce financial risk)
- ✅ Authentic representation (diversity through real people)
- ✅ Creative empowerment (multiply impact of one shoot)
- ✅ Sustainability focus (reduce waste in fashion)
